Shipping, Packaging & Care

How is the art packaged?

Lippan art is extremely fragile due to the delicate mud work and mirrors. We take utmost care in packaging. Each piece is first wrapped in butter paper to protect the clay, followed by multiple layers of high-density bubble wrap. It is then secured in a custom-fitted wooden or heavy-duty corrugated box with foam corners to prevent any movement during transit.

How long does shipping take?

Since each piece is handcrafted, standard pieces take about 7-10 days to prepare before shipping. Custom commissions can take 2-3 weeks depending on the size and complexity. Once shipped, delivery within India takes 3-5 business days.

How do I care for my Lippan art?

To ensure the longevity of your piece:

  • Keep it away from direct sunlight to prevent the clay colors from fading.
  • Avoid placing it in highly humid areas like bathrooms.
  • Clean gently with a soft, dry brush or microfiber cloth. Never use water or liquid cleaners on the mud work.
  • Mirrors can be cleaned lightly with a slightly damp cotton swab if absolutely necessary.
